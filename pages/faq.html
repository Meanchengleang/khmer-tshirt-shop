Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering

How do I place an order?

Select your desired t-shirt, choose size and color, then click "Add to Cart". Follow the checkout process to complete your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal.

Can I modify my order?

Orders can be modified within 1 hour of placement. Contact customer service for assistance.

Shipping & Delivery

How long will my order take to arrive?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping takes 1-2 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International delivery typically takes 7-14 business days.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer 30-day returns for unworn items in original packaging.

How do I return an item?

Contact customer service for a return label and follow the instructions provided.

Product Information

How do I care for my t-shirt?

Machine wash cold, tumble dry low. Do not bleach. Iron if needed.

What materials are used?

Our t-shirts are made from 100% premium cotton for maximum comfort.
